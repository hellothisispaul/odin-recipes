Old Fashioned

Description

The Old Fashioned is a classic cocktail for whiskey lovers.

Made with whiskey, sugar, and angostura bitters the Old Fashioned is a tale as old as time, one of the six basic drinks listed in David A. Embury's The Fine Art of Mixing Drinks.

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz whiskey (bourbon or rye, as preferred)
  • 1/2 oz simple syrup
  • 2 dashes angostura bitters
  • orange peel for garnish

Steps:

  1. Combine whiskey, simple syrup, and bitters in mixing glass
  2. Fill mixing glass with ice and stir for 10-15 seconds
  3. Strain cocktail into rocks glass over fresh ice
  4. Rim glass with orange peel, twist peel over glass and place twisted peel into the finished cocktail
